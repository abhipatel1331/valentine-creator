A romantic and elegant Valentine's website with playful interactions and beautiful animations.

## Pages & Flow

### 1. Landing Page - "Be My Valentine?"
- **Hero Section**: Elegant display with "Foram, Will You Be My Valentine?" text
- **Interactive Buttons**:
  - **Yes Button**: Slowly grows bigger when hovered, encouraging her to click
  - **No Button**: Playfully runs away when she tries to click it (moves to random positions)
- **Floating Animations**: Hearts and sparkles floating gently across the screen
- **Color Palette**: Classic red and pink with elegant cream/white accents

### 2. Success Page - Love Letter
- Appears after clicking "Yes" 
- **Personalized Love Letter Section**: A beautifully styled letter/message area where you can write your heartfelt message to Foram
- **Celebration Animation**: Burst of hearts and sparkles when she says yes
- Smooth transition with romantic entrance animation

### 3. Photo Gallery Section
- **Elegant Photo Gallery**: Display your favorite photos together
- **Romantic Styling**: Polaroid-style or elegant frames with hover effects
- Will start with placeholder images that you can easily replace with your own photos

## Design Elements
- **Typography**: Elegant script/cursive fonts for headings, clean fonts for body
- **Colors**: Rich reds (#DC143C, #FF1744), soft pinks (#FFB6C1, #FFC0CB), cream whites
- **Animations**: 
  - Floating hearts continuously in background
  - Sparkle effects scattered throughout
  - Smooth hover transitions on all interactive elements

## Technical Approach
- Single-page app with smooth scrolling between sections
- No backend needed - all content stored in the code
- Mobile-friendly responsive design
